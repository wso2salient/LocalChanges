TTB is improving your online experience

As a part of our technology modernization effort, TTB is streamlining your online experience. We are implementing "Single Sign On" which means you'll only need one password to access all of your TTB applications. However, this transition will take some time. Existing users should continue to log into each application as they normally do.

Check back here and watch for communications as this effort continues.

View our 2018-2022 Stratgic Plan to learn more about TTB's efforts to facilitate commerce and modernize the labeling process.

✔
A single username and password for all your TTB applications.
✔
The ability to create and manage users for your organization.
✔
Improved security controls to protect your data.
✔
A more consistent look and feel across applications.
