OCTOBER 5, 2019

New Conditionally Approved Status in COLAs.

Now TTB can propose minor changes to your application so it matches the label(s) you submitted, and send it back to you as “Conditionally Approved.” You have 7 days to accept or decline the changes. For details, check out What’s New in COLAs Online 4.7 (PDF).
OCTOBER 5, 2019

Having trouble getting Formulas Online to work properly?

It is possible that you may have a pop-up blocker running as part of your web browser settings. You must turn off the pop-up blocker in order for Formulas Online to operate properly. Please see How to Allow Pop-Ups in Internet Explorer 11 (PDF) for more information.
