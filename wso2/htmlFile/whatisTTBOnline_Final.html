What is TTB Online?

TTB Online is a single, secure place where you can apply for and update your permit, formula, and label applications. Explore our online systems:

Permits Online helps you apply for and update the permits, registrations, or notices you need to operate a TTB-regulated business. Learn more

Formulas Online helps you apply for and update formula and sample submissions for alcohol beverages and nonbeverage products. Learn more

COLAs Online helps you apply for certificates of label approval (COLA), certificates of exemption from label approval, and distinctive liquor bottle approvals. Learn more
